About Me

I am a leader in software organizations with experience ranging from development, implementation, deployment, to support. My background in each of these areas gives perspective and informs my mindful but ambitious attitude. The interesting part about me isn't the variety in my experience, but the evolution of the motivation behind it. Over time, I shifted my focus from what I was doing to why I was doing it. I was keenly aware and interested in the end-user experience of my individual or my team's work and found that the best were ones where the user could experience a feeling of joy. I would think of ways to make the user think, "Oh, I like that!" when they would use whatever I was working on. It seemed like a very simple idea, but not one that was often talked about in a room full of developers and operations folk. By combining my technical experience with my passion for understanding users and their motivation, I have been able to make a marked improvement in each organization I joined. Just a few of my successes include decreasing the response time and increasing the resolution rate of operations issues at KWI, increasing uptime and stability of services at Deutsche Bank, and automating and innovating development and deployment workflows at IPsoft. I am currently working on several different software projects (some of which are detailed below!) as well as working full time for a software company in New York City (again, check out the details below). Feel free to reach out and follow me on the social media at the very bottom of the page, or use the contact form sitting right there to drop me an email.
